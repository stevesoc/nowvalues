The short version: NowValues.com is owned and operated by Strategic Online Corp. We collect minimal data, we don’t sell it, we don’t use cookies for tracking, and we’re straight with you about affiliate relationships.

1. Who We Are

NowValues.com (“NowValues,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) is a product review and recommendation website owned and operated by Strategic Online Corp. Our website is located at https://nowvalues.com. If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can reach us at privacy@nowvalues.com.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information when you visit NowValues.com:

a) Analytics data (automatically collected)
We use Cloudflare Web Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. Cloudflare Web Analytics is a privacy-first, cookie-free analytics tool. It collects:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website (where you came from)
  • Approximate geographic location (country/region, derived from anonymized IP address)
  • Browser type, operating system, and device category
  • Core Web Vitals performance metrics (page load times, etc.)

Cloudflare does not use cookies, does not track you across websites, and does not build individual visitor profiles. Data is aggregated and anonymized. For more information, see Cloudflare’s Privacy Policy.

b) Information you provide voluntarily
If you contact us via email or any future contact form, we collect the information you choose to provide (such as your name and email address) solely to respond to your inquiry.

c) Information we do NOT collect
We do not collect payment information, create user accounts, or require registration to use this site.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

NowValues.com does not set any first-party cookies. Our analytics provider (Cloudflare) operates without cookies.

However, when you click an affiliate or external link, the destination website may set its own cookies and collect data according to its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party sites you visit.

4. Affiliate Links and Disclosure

NowValues.com participates in affiliate marketing programs. This means that some links on our site are affiliate links: if you click one and make a purchase, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you.

Our affiliate relationships include, but may not be limited to, programs operated by Amazon, and other retailers and brands. Affiliate link clicks may be tracked by the respective merchant or affiliate network using cookies or other tracking technologies governed by their own privacy policies.

All sponsored or affiliate content is clearly disclosed. Our editorial opinions are not influenced by affiliate relationships — we only recommend products we genuinely believe provide value.

We use the information we collect to:
